ShiftShare is an app designed to access a pool of potential employees in the same field

Created By Amber Gupta, Brennan Jones, Murray Estes, and Carter Yancey

A problem that All managers face...

Inspiration for the Solution - "ShiftShare"

Big companies like CVS, Walgreen maintain their pharmacist work schedule through a centralized process. So when one employee takes unexpected breaks, they have backups ready! While on the other hand, independent business like Convenience Stores and Franchise businesses like Subway, McDonalds etc do not have this ability. Owners have to deal with unplanned employee scheduling situation on a regular basis. "ShiftShare" is an answer to this problem.

How are we different from traditional players like Craigslist?

Instead of allowing just anybody to sign up, it requires Managers to list their employees in return for accessing our pool. Given another manager's list of employees and these employees' schedules, "ShiftShare" can provide a manager in an exigent situation with a list of verified employees near his location.

A WIN - WIN Situation for everyone!

ShiftShare creates a Win-Win situation because minimum wage employers get chance to be paid more during unplanned work while managers can now trust the new person who is going to represent their businesses.

Features

Manager Portal

Allow managers to add his employees, search for employees to see their availability & rate them

Employee Portal

The employee portal allows employess to login and submit their available work hours

Communication Platform

Integration with the phone's native text function to facilitate communication between employees and managers.

Profile Managment & Ratings

Allows managers and employeers to rate each other

MultiLayer Search

Allows managers to search for potential employees to cover shifts
